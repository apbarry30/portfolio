Alessia's Lights VR

Alessia's Lights is
a VR learning experience about the warning signs of emotional domestic abuse

the Inspiration beings here


How can we get college students to understand
and recall information about the early warning signs, severity and parameters surrounding an
emotionally abusive relationship?

LEARN

How does VR encourage this? Concerns of this age group include relationships with friends and social status.

EXPERIENCE

Even though VR technology is new, it is easy for this generation to understand and excites them.

MOTIVATE

Students will have better recall and memory - retention when learning in an immersive environment.

Why VR?
The joining of information and experience is likely to lead to deeper and more durable knowledge. As I found in studies. It contributes to behavior change.

What are the warning signs?

Explore the 5 warning signs of an unhealthy relationship that are featured in the film. If you are seeing unhealthy signs in your relationship, it’s important to not ignore them and understand they can escalate to abuse.

When someone expresses very extreme feelings and over-the-top behavior that feels overwhelming.

Intensity

Isolation could mean that your partner takes away your independence, pulling you away from your family and friends.

Isolation

When someone is jealous to a point where they try to control who you spend time with and what you do.

Extreme Jealousy

Volatility within an unhealthy relationship can look like constant ups and downs, as well as unpredictable and shocking behavior.

Volatlity

When someone does and says things to make you feel bad about yourself.

Disrespect

Development

This experience was created adobe premier pro, Unity Engine and c#. Alessia's story is based on a true experience, translated into a Virtual Reality experience for the Oculus Quest. Now, Alessia can tell her story directly to YOU.

From Interview to Filming

Gathering information to build this experience came from interviews of real victims and survivors of emotionally abuse. This can enlighten us to a real experience. Now we can all say we know someone that is a survivor, Alessia. Inspired by the true story, Alessia's Lights was created into a film script.

to VR

Filming behind a transparent background is essentail to obtain the transparency needed for a virtual reality experience. I have taken Alessia back to her college dorm room, and now you are right across from her exploring the signs she missed.
